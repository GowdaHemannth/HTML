Captain America is a Marvel superhero who represents courage, justice, and patriotism. His real name is Steve Rogers, a weak but brave young man who volunteers for a secret military experiment during World War II. The experiment turns him into a super-soldier with enhanced strength, speed, and endurance. Captain America is known for his iconic shield made of vibranium, which he uses both for defense and combat. Even though he has superhuman abilities, his greatest strength is his strong moral values—he always stands up for what is right, even when it’s difficult. He is a founding member of The Avengers and often acts as their leader. Captain America believes in teamwork, sacrifice, and protecting innocent people.
